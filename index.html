Human Capital Enterprise Operating System

A federated decision-support architecture built to prioritize The Marine over the system.

Capability is Produced — Not Managed

The Marine Corps manages people, money, and training in isolation. This creates friction for the individual and risk for the Commander. HCEOS is the connective tissue that integrates these disparate domains into a single Marine-centric reality.

The "How": Federated Architecture

Layer 1: The Backbone

A unified ontology centered on the Marine. We link the person to their requirement, their capability, and their resource constraints across all domains.

Layer 2: Decision Intelligence

Instead of dashboards, we build Decision Objects. We forecast risk in assignments, boards, and retention before they impact readiness.

Authoritative Systems (MCTFS, MCTIMS) remain protected. HCEOS is the intelligence layer on top.
